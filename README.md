# Hi, I'm Shannon 👋

**Technical Product & Project Manager for sports, health, and wellness tech.** You bring the what and the why. I build the how, and I manage it to done.

Tell me what you're trying to build, and somewhere in the middle of you talking, my head is already putting the systems together. I can't really help it. That's the part I'm built for.

I've spent more than a decade inside youth sports, building recreation, club, and wellness programs from the ground up. Now I turn that operator experience into products: my own, and my clients'. When I come into your build, I've sat in your chair.

## 🛠️ What I do

Two crafts, one person. The **product** side: strategy, positioning, MVP scoping, data models, and the pricing and sequencing decisions that make a build worth doing. The **project** side: turning that into shipped software, backlogs run as launch gates, specs with acceptance criteria, QA designed as the source of truth, releases gated and verified. I work solo or embedded with founders, end to end, and I'm equally at home starting a build from zero or taking one over mid-stream and managing it to done.

I run AI-native: strategy and specs in chat, implementation by a coding agent, every diff reviewed before merge, and git, schema, and deploys kept human-only. With AI in the loop, the scarce skill isn't generation, it's verification, and that's the part I own.
